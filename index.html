About Rapids Express

Rapids Express is a chain of express car washes located in Orange County, California. Our mission is to deliever quality washes at affordable prices. We have been operating since 2018 at our Orange site, and plan on expanding in the coming years. We provide four types of washes, starting as low as $5.00! The washes range from a standard wash, all the way to a wash, wax, and red hot lava combo! We also offer membership deals at affordable prices.

Our Locations

We currently have one location open. It is located on 2045 N Tustin Street in Orange, California. In the coming years, we will be expanding to Fullerton, California as well.
